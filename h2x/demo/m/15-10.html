    For Windows users, click on StartControl Panel SystemRemote Desktop. Check the box that says "Allow users to connect remotely to this computer." Write down the full name of your work computer. You will need this later when you connect from home.    Nov 10, 2019

    How to Access Your Work Computer from Home (with Pictures)

    Set Up Work Computer
    1. Click the "Start" button and right-click "Computer," and then select "Properties."
    2. Click the "Remote Settings" menu and select the "Remote" tab. Check the "Allow Remote Assistance Connections to This Computer" option.
    3. Click "Select Users" and "Add" in the Remote Desktop Users dialog box.
