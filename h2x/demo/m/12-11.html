    Description

    Checkmate is a game position in chess and other chess-like games in which a player's king is in check and there is no way to remove the threat. Checkmating the opponent wins the game. In chess, the king is never capturedthe game ends as soon as the king is checkmated. Wikipedia

    Checkmate - Wikipedia
    Checkmate (often shortened to mate) is a game position in chess and other chess-like games in which a player's king is in check (threatened with capture ) and there is no way to remove the threat. Checkmating the opponent wins the game.
