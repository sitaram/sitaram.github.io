    Description

    The French Defence is a chess opening characterised by the moves: 1. e4 e6 This is most commonly followed by 2.d4 d5, with Black intending ...c5 at a later stage, attacking White's centre and gaining space on the queenside. Wikipedia
    Moves: 1.e4 e6
    ECO: C00C19
    Named after: London vs. Paris correspondence match (18341836)
