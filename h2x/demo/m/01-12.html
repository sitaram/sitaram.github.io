    Pay As You Earn is an income-driven repayment plan that caps federal student loan payments at 10% of your discretionary income and forgives your remaining balance after 20 years of repayment. You'll likely qualify for PAYE if you can't afford your payments and didn't start college until after 2007.Apr 9, 2019
