      noun

      noun: stalemate
      1. a position counting as a draw, in which a player is not in check but cannot move except into check.
      verb
      verb: stalemate; 3rd person present: stalemates; past tense: stalemated; past participle: stalemated; gerund or present participle: stalemating
      1. bring to or cause to reach stalemate.
