      Forbearance

      Description

      Forbearance, in the context of a mortgage process, is a special agreement between the lender and the borrower to delay a foreclosure. The literal meaning of forbearance is holding back. When mortgage borrowers are unable to meet their repayment terms, lenders may opt to foreclose. Wikipedia
