Educational Outcomes Extensions

CollegeOrUniversityWithOutcomes CollegeOrUniversityWithOutcomes is the google extension of CollegeOrUniversity that adds educational outcome data. Subclass of: CollegeOrUniversity
EducationalFacet EducationalOutcome is the google extension which allows for specifying a single piece of educational outcome data, such as the company a former student works for. Subclass of: Intangible
fieldOfStudyFacet Specifies that the educational outcome data is for a particular field of study. Domain: CollegeOrUniversityWithOutcomes Range: EducationalFacet
companyFacet Specifies that the educational outcome data is for a particular company. Domain: CollegeOrUniversityWithOutcomes Range: EducationalFacet
functionFacet Specifies that the educational outcome data is for a particular occupation. Domain: CollegeOrUniversityWithOutcomes Range: EducationalFacet
EducationalOutcome EducationalOutcome is the google extension which allows for specifying the number of outcomes matching a single piece of outcome data, such as a specific field of study. Subclass of: EducationalFacet
fieldOfStudyOutcomes Educational outcome data by field of study. Domain: CollegeOrUniversityWithOutcomes Range: EducationalOutcome
companyOutcomes Educational outcome data by company of employment. Domain: CollegeOrUniversityWithOutcomes Range: EducationalOutcome
functionOutcomes Educational outcome data by occupation. Domain: CollegeOrUniversityWithOutcomes Range: EducationalOutcome
numStudents Count of matching educational outcomes. Domain: CollegeOrUniversityWithOutcomes Domain: EducationalOutcome Range: Integer
